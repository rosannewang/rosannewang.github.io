ABOUT ME


Rosanne Wang was born in New Haven, Connecticut, but has spent most of her life in Richmond, Virginia. She quickly developed a passion for art because of her mother who was an artist. Throughout middle school and the beginning of high school, she focused on honing her technical art skills. As the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020 hit and life became virtual, she started creating works for a purpose instead of technique. Since then, Wang has continued to explore what art means to her personally.

Outside of the visual arts, Wang enjoys many other art forms. She enjoys singing and playing instruments and loves to run and weightlift. Currently, she lives in and attends school in Virginia but hopes to get a new perspective and move elsewhere in the future.
