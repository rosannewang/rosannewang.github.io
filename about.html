ABOUT ME

Rosanne Wang was born in New Haven, Connecticut and spent most of her early childhood in a semi-rural city upstate New York. She later moved to Virginia to finish elementary, start middle, and later attend high school. Rosanne's interest in art developed at a young age with the help of her mother who was also an artist. As a observant thoughtful girl, Rosanne found that she had countless ideas and a unique take on the world that she could only express through her creations. Today, she creates her artworks in Virginia where she also attends high school.
