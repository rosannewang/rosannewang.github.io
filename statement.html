STATEMENT

Why does everything have to be the way it is? Why does art have to be a certain way to look "right"? I find that in my art, I often try to make special efforts to avoid some sort of norm or social construct or try to express an idea or emotion that I feel or think of that can't be described with words. Many of my works stem from my intuition or bursts of emotion, but I do sometimes enjoy drawing or painting things the way they are. Through the years, I have found that for me, art is a hobby, a form of therapy, entertainment, a compulsion, and sometimes even a competition, but more importantly, art is my way coming to an equilibrium with the bizarre world around me and the ways I think, feel, and exist.
